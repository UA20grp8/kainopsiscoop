Kainopsiscoop

The tunnel symbolizes the road we travel as a person in which the kaleidoscope, just like the corona crisis, forms a tipping point. You enter it unexpectedly and without looking for it, you get time and space to reflect on yourself, about the world and your position in it.

The passage of the kaleidoscope starts out narrow so you will enter the tunnel as an individual. However, the further you move through it, the wider the kaleidoscope becomes and so does your view of the world. The space shows a false-looking reflection of reality, this alienation from the physical world gives you a new view of the future.

In the distance you see the people who have traveled the same path before you. They are, as it were, in an 'exalted' state. When you leave the kaleidoscope, you too become an exalted version of yourself, a self that will look at the world with a more conscious view.
