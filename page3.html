Titel project

Today we live in an accelerated society, exacerbated by the corona crisis in the field of flexibility and digitization. This suppresses the individual's progress, slowing it down. We are heading towards what is ment to be ‘normal’ that we have unconsciously imposed on ourselves.
Our installation strives to break through the associations we make between standing still and lagging behind in order to demonstrate that standing still creates space for self-development and reflection. Only this self-reflection leads to the development of the collective. In this way we turn the emotional stagnation into a possibility for real progress.

Kaleidoscope

The tunnel symbolizes the road we travel as a person in which the kaleidoscope, like the corona crisis, is a tipping point. You enter it unexpectedly and without looking for it, you get time and space to reflect.

The passage starts narrow through which you enter the tunnel as an individual. However, the further you move through it, the wider the kaleidoscope and so does your view.

The new space you are in shows a false-looking reflection of reality. This alienation from the physical world gives you a new perspective on the future.

In the distance you see the people who have come the same way before you. They are, as it were, in an 'exalted' state. When you leave the kaleidoscope, you too become an exalted version of yourself.
